Careers @ LBSMDC Parappanagadi


  • Faculty

    Presently the Lal Bahadhur Shastri Model Degree College, Parappanagadi is being mentored by LBSCENTRE, Thiruvanathapuram. Being the academic mentor, faculty recruitment for LBS-MDC is also managed by LBSCENTRE. The institute invites applications from motivated candidates with research expertise in all areas of Computer Science and Information Technology,Commerce researchers. Suitable candidates must have a relevant NET,SET,B.ed qualification in a relevant discipline. We have openings at the levels of Assistant Professor, Associate Professor, and Professor. Visiting faculty positions are also available for short or long term visits. To apply, please submit a curriculum Resume, a publication list, a one-page summary of research plans, a statement of teaching interests, and the names of three references (with contact information) electronically to lbsiistpgimdc@mail.com
