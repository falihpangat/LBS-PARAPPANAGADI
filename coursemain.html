The BSC Computer Science is a three year bachelor's degree which deals with subjects and topics related to computer application and service.In genaral,Computer science degree programs emphasize the mathematical and theoretical foundations of computing.

BCA
Computer Application

BCA Bachelor in Computer Application (BCA) is an undergraduate degree course in computer applications. It comprises of the subjects like database, networking, data structure, core programming languages like ‘C’ and ‘java’.
