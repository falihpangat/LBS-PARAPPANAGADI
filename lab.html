    LAB



    The primary aim of LBS Parappanagadi Library is to support research, teaching and learning of the institute community. The Library is one of the central support services of LBSMDC Parappanagadi. The mission of the Library is to provide information services and access to original full text printed resources to support the scholarly and informational needs of faculty, students and staff. The Library is in fast growing stage. It was started in December 2016 with approximately 350 books. In March 2017 Library Bacame more expanded and it usefull for the scholars . During this short span of time, the library has acquired a good number of books and computer resources. The library has a good collection of books on Computer Science, Information Technology, Economics, Mathematics, Statistical studies & Business and also technical and general print magazines. The collection includes reference books, text books, Records etc. We are constantly in a process of increasing our collection of books further.

    Students must follow the rules and regulations mentioned in the Library Circulation Policy.
